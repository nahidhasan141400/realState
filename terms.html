Privacy Policy

Introduction

Radiant Living Ltd. is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing or using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.


Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you fill out our contact form, including your name, mobile number, email address, and query. We may also collect non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.


How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to respond to your query and provide you with information about our real estate services. We may also use your information to improve our website and services and to communicate with you about updates, promotions, and other relevant information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements. We may disclose your information to our agents, contractors, and service providers who assist us in providing our services, or as required by law.


Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also withdraw your consent for us to collect and use your information at any time. To exercise these rights or to request more information about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@radiantlivingltd.com, radiantlivingltd@gmail.com.
