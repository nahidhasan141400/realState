Join Our Team As a

Accounts Officer

Radiant Living Limited, a leading real estate development company, is seeking an experienced and highly skilled Account Officer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing all financial transactions, analyzing financial data, preparing reports, and ensuring compliance with accounting standards and regulations.

Responsibilities:
  • Manage all accounting transactions related to real estate development, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger.
  • Reconcile bank statements, credit card statements, and other financial records related to real estate development
  • Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports related to real estate development.
  • Analyze financial data related to real estate development and identify trends and patterns
  • Develop and implement accounting policies and procedures to ensure compliance with real estate accounting standards and regulations
  • Process invoices and payments related to real estate development.
  • Participate in audits and provide documentation as needed
  • Monitor cash flow related to real estate development and prepare cash flow projections
  • Provide financial advice to other departments to support real estate development budgeting and financial planning.
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Proven work experience as an Account Officer in a real estate development company or similar role.
  • Strong knowledge of real estate accounting principles, standards, and regulations.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Proficiency in MS Office, especially Excel.
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

If you meet the above requirements and are interested in joining our team at Radiant Living Limited, please submit your resume and cover letter. We look forward to hearing from you!
